BAMM.tv

Responsive Web Design

Summer & Fall 2014

Summary

In the spring of 2014, I was hired as a Web Consultant for Bricks and Mortar Media (BAMM.tv), a multimedia content creator that specializes in mixing, mastering, and filming live music. Their mission is to empower Indie artists through their tailored process of music video creation and distribution. I worked with company executives and the creative team to build a B2B website that conveys their company’s mission and capabilities to new business leads. The ultimate goal of this single page site was to persuade potential partners to do business with BAMM’s boutique studio rather than corporate giants in a monolithic industry.

In the early stages of our design process, I worked closely with the Founder and Media Director to define the core values and capabilities of the company. These elements heavily influenced the content strategy as we moved forward with storyboarding and art direction. After completing the content strategy and site architecture we moved forward with mock-ups and comps of the site. The aesthetic of the site was inspired by concert posters with vibrant colors, contrast, and bold headlines. I also created illustrations and animations that accompanied key components of the site such as the sales pitch which describes BAMM.tv’s solution to music licensing and content delivery.
