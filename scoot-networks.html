Scoot Networks

Mobile App / Prototyping

Winter 2014

Self-Initiated Prompt

I'm a huge fan of Scoot Networks, a company that facilitates efficient and reliable transportation via shared, electric scooters. I have taken hundreds of scoot rides and I have had a blast zipping through San Francisco in zero-emission electric mopeds. Although I love the work that Scoot team has released so far, I think that Scoot's user interface could be greatly improved. Along with the UI/UX shortcomings, I felt that the transportation platform is missing out on their incredible potential for utilizing ride data to enrich the user experience. Taking this into consideration, I recreated the application and added several features that make it easier for users to carry out essential tasks such as looking up parking info, viewing location data, and booking a reservation. After building a prototype, I sent it over to Scoot HQ for further review.
